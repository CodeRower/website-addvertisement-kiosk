Digital Signage Software for Kiosks

Cloud-based digital signage may be a web-based platform that permits you to remotely create, update and manage the digital kiosks content. Additionally to content management of the digital kiosk, digital signage software allows you to remotely view the status of all content playing on the digital kiosk, run proof-of-play reports, online connectivity of the media player. This monitoring makes troubleshooting easier plus provides you with valuable data of your playlists.
