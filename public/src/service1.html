Types of Digital Kiosk

Digital kiosks are free-standing, self-contained units. they are available in both portrait and landscape orientation screen options and may be bespoke to possess a sleek style that's eye-catching while adding to the locations ambiance.

  • Interactive digital kiosks in delhi are wont to create immersive and interesting user experiences
  • Special table top digital kiosks are available ideal for retail and POP settings
  • Single sided digital kiosks are typically available in 32”, 43”, 48” and 55” sizes
  • Double and triple sided digital kiosks also are available but are usually custom built
  • Outdoor digital kiosks are specially built to face up to weather.
