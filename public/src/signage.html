What is Digital Signage

Digital signage may be a sub-segment of electronic signage. Digital displays use technologies like LCD, LED, projection and e-paper to display digital images, video, web pages, weather data, restaurant menus, or text. They will be found publicly spaces, transportation systems, museums, stadiums, retail stores, hotels, restaurants and company buildings etc., to supply way-finding, exhibitions, marketing and outdoor advertising. They are used as a network of electronic displays that are centrally managed and individually addressable for the display of text, animated or video messages for advertising, information, entertainment and merchandising to targeted audiences.
